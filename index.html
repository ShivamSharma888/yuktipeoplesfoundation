About Us

Youkti People’s Foundation is committed to education, equality, and community welfare. Our mission is to support villages, promote logical thinking, and empower youth through service and education initiatives.

Our Impact

Educational Support

We have helped children from rural and underprivileged backgrounds gain access to quality education, books, and mentorship. Our volunteers organize study camps, awareness drives, and logical learning workshops to strengthen foundational education in villages.

Community Service

Through community engagement programs, cleanliness drives, support for elderly citizens, and village welfare activities, we aim to build a strong environment of unity and progress. Our team actively participates in helping people during emergencies and social challenges.

Youth Empowerment

Our foundation regularly conducts motivational events, leadership training sessions, and career guidance seminars for the youth. We believe in empowering young minds to build logical thinking, self-confidence, and a sense of responsibility toward society.
